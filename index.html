The Wedding

Ceremony & Reception

We are getting married at CJ's Off The Square. We are getting married at CJ's Off The Square in Franklin, TN. The ceremony will take place in the garden, followed by a reception in the pavilion and house, which was buil in 1911 and is on the National Register of Historic Places.

  • Sunday - October 9th, 2016
  • 4:30pm - 10:00pm
  • CJ's Off The Square

Lodging

We have reserved a block of rooms at Franklin Marriott Cool Springs, 10 minutes from the venue and 25 minutes from downtown Nashville. Please book by 9/3/16 to ensure a room with a discounted rate, the rooms are reserved under the Kelly/Cournoyer, $119/night for October 8th & 9th. If you wish to add additional nights to your stay, please call the hotel directly for rates and information.

  • Saturday - October to Monday - October 10th, 2016
  • 700 Cool Springs Blvd, Franklin, TN 37067
